Demon Slayer

Tanjirou finds his family slaughtered and the lone survivor, his sister Nezuko, turned into a demon.Thus begins Tanjirou's quest to fight demons and turn his sister human again.

Death Note

One day Light Yagami finds the Death Note, a notebook held by a shinigami (Death God). With the Death Note in hand, Light decides to create a perfect world. A world without crime or criminals.

One Piece

Pirate Gol D. Roger obtained wealth, fame, and power to earn the title of Pirate King.Twenty-two years after Gol D. Roger's death, a boy named Monkey D. Luffy decided to become a pirate and search for Gol D. Roger's treasure to become the next Pirate King.

Fullmetal Alchemist

While trying to resurrect their dead mother using alchemy the Edward loses left leg and arm while his brother loses his whole body. Now Edward vows to search for the Philosopher’s Stone to return him and his brother to their original bodies.

Attack on Titan

As the city walls were destroyed by the mysterious titans Eren and Mikasa witnessed their mother eaten alive by the titan.Eren vows that he will murder every single giant and take revenge for all of mankind.

Naruto

The series follows a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, who strives to become the Hokage of his village, the Hidden Leaf. Although he has the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed inside him and is shunned because of it, he works hard to become strong.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuuji finds himself suddenly thrust into the world of Curses—dreadful beings formed from human malice and negativity—after swallowing the said item, revealed to be a finger belonging to the demon Sukuna Ryoumen, the "King of Curses.

Hunter x Hunter

Believing that he will meet his father by becoming a Hunter, Gon takes the first step to walk the same path.During the Hunter Examination, Gon befriends Leorio Paladiknight, Kurapika and ex-assassin Killua.

Dr Stone

a blinding green light strikes the Earth and petrifies mankind around the world—turning every single human into stone.Several millennia later, Senku and Taiju freed and are trying to rebuild modern society with science

Chainsaw Man

Denji now a Devil guided by the promise of a content life alongside an attractive woman, devotes everything and fights with all his might as a devil hunter to make his naive dreams a reality.

Your Name

Kimi no Na wa. revolves around Mitsuha and Taki's actions after they woke up in each other's body, weaving them into a fabric held together by fate and circumstance.

Bleach

To save his family, Ichigo accepts Rukia's offer of taking her powers and becomes a Soul Reaper as a result. As he fights hollows he soon learns that the Hollows are not the only real threat to the human world.
